¡Welcome to ROVOFIC!

We are an extracurricular robotics club with an emphasis on Mechatronics

What is our objetive?

Rovofic, strengthen with face-to-face or virtual activities the fields of Industry 4.0, mechatronics engineering, robotics, electronics, and 3D design and printing.

How do we do it?

We generate new skills in children and adolescents through our methodology validated for 6 years, directed by fields of psychology, pedagogy and mechatronics.

What can you learn?

You will develop cognitive-logical mathematical, emotional and social skills such as: identification and solution of problems, frustration tolerance, and creativity.

Dare to learn by building robots!
